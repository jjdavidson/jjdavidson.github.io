Hello and Welcome.

My name is Jonathan, and I am a graduate student at California State University Los Angeles (CSULA) pursuing a Master's degree in Mathematics. I serve as the Math Gym Coordinator at CSULA which supports students taking Precalculus and Calculus. I also teach Precalculus classes at CSULA. In spring 2025 I will be teaching Math 1085: Precalculus for Life Sciences. Outside of CSULA, I work as an instructor at Elite Prep teaching SAT Math, AP Calculus, AP Statistics, AP Physics, and AP Chemistry.

Research Interests

My research interests are in combinatorics. In particular, I like to study hypergraphs in the context of extremal combinatorics and the probabilistic method. Recently, I have been focusing on the Erdos Box Problem, Odd Coverings of Hypergraphs, and the Union-Closed Sets Conjecture under the tutelage of Jason O'Neill at CSULA.
