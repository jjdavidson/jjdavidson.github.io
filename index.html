My name is Jonathan, and I am a mathematician.

This blog documents unsolved math problems I work on in my spare time. Each of these problems are of the "Easy to state, hard to solve" type, so each problem should be accessible to a wide audience of math enthusiasts. For each problem, I will provide historical and mathematical background as well as present recent progress towards a solution. The goal of this blog is to organize and share my research into these various problems, and challenge other budding mathematicians to work on these problems.
